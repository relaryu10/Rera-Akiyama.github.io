I'm Rera Akiyama, a Film, TV and Multimedia student at Sungkyunkwan University. My creative practice lives in the quiet space between a moment and its memory — photography as a way of holding still what is always moving.

I enjoy capturing everyday moments and crafting simple, aesthetic images that speak without noise. Visual design, to me, is the art of intentional restraint.
